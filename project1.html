A Book About Loneliness

This project was one of a series of books created by my Typography 1 class in which each book would explore a different social issue. I chose the issue of loneliness, which is a more widespread and consequential problem than many people may realize and which I was struggling with myself at the time. The aim was to focus mainly on typographical elements, with any images or graphics being a secondary consideration.
